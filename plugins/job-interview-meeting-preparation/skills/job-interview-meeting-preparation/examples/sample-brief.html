Lead with cross-portfolio operating fluency. "NorthBay and Helios were in commercial relationship before Westfield owned Helios; the founder was an early angel and NorthBay was a customer. I came up inside the Westfield thesis on the regulated-data side."
Five-acquisition integration parallel. The new add-on is Helios's first cross-border integration. You led product diligence and PMI on five at NorthBay, including AI/ML capability mapping. Precise match.
Customer-facing AI in regulated environments. Same pattern Helios is running with AI scoring, Agent Studio, Trust Center. You've already done the AI-meets-regulated-buyer translation problem.
The 6x AI adoption data point as a probe. "That kind of curve usually means either the product caught up to demand or customers are now feeling the trust ceiling. Where is the team focused next?"
Distributed-team operating cadence. Helios runs four global offices; you ran 12 PMs and 119 engineers across three time zones with sustained EU-India overlap. State as a fact, not a brag.
