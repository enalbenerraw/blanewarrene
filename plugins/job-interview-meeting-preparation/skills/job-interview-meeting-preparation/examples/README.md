# Sample Output

This directory contains a sanitized worked example of what the skill
produces. All names, companies, and details are fictional and chosen
to mirror the structural shape of a real PE-portfolio interview prep
without identifying real people.

## The scenario

- **Candidate (the user)**: a former VP Product at NorthBay Trust, a
  fictional financial-services-adjacent compliance company, looking
  at VP and CPO seats.
- **Stakeholder**: Taylor Brooks, Senior Executive Recruiter at
  Westfield Capital (fictional PE firm).
- **Company being recruited for**: Helios Compliance Systems, a
  long-held Westfield portfolio company in the middle of an AI
  productization arc.
- **Hot signal**: Taylor cited the candidate's NorthBay tenure
  unprompted in initial outreach. Both companies are in the
  Westfield portfolio.
- **Meeting type**: initial recruiter screen, Tuesday 10:00 AM, Zoom.

## Files

- `sample-brief.html`: the populated one-pager.
- `sample-brief.pdf`: Letter portrait, print-ready.
- `sample-brief.png`: high-resolution screenshot (used in the
  repository README).

The full in-conversation brief that accompanies the one-pager is not
reproduced here. In normal use, the brief renders in the chat itself
and the one-pager is the only persistent artifact.
