# Product in Acquisitions OS

A practical operating system for product leaders accountable for post-acquisition integration outcomes. Built on the 90-Day Product Integration Framework by Blane Warrene.

## What this plugin does

When you install this plugin, Claude becomes an integration partner that runs you through the four phases of post-close product integration:

1. **Week 0 readiness.** Name the integration lead, draft decision rights, build the combined product narrative, inventory customer commitments, and pass the five-question Day 1 readiness gate.
2. **Roadmap convergence.** Facilitate the readout, conflict diagnosis, and decision sessions. Categorize conflicts as vision, sequencing, or scope. Produce a merged 90-day plus 12-month roadmap and a 30/60/90 plan.
3. **Positioning stability.** Answer the "what can a customer do now that they could not before" question. Generate the sales FAQ, talk track, competitive response scripts, top-20 outreach plan, and advisory council charter.
4. **People integration.** Design decision map sessions, mentor pairings, 30/60/90 ownership plans, retention risk reviews framed around purpose and influence, and a 1:1 coaching question library.

Each skill produces real, reusable artifacts you can paste into Notion, Confluence, or a slide.

## Who this is for

Acquiring-company product leaders (VP Product, Head of Product, CPO) who own integration outcomes. Also useful for Corp Dev leads, integration program managers, and GTM leaders who need product-aligned messaging through a transition.

## How to use it

After installing, ask Claude any of these:

- "I just got named the product integration lead. Help me get ready for Day 1."
- "Run me through Week 0 readiness."
- "Facilitate a roadmap conflict diagnosis."
- "Build me a positioning statement for the combined company."
- "Design the first 90 days for our acquired product team."

Claude will load the relevant skill and walk you through the framework step by step, producing the deliverables along the way.

## Installation

1. In Cowork or Claude Code, open the plugin install panel.
2. Drop in the `product-in-acquisitions-os.plugin` file.
3. Confirm the install.

The four skills become available immediately.

## About the framework

The 90-Day Product Integration Framework is a five-lever system: ownership, unified product vision, roadmap convergence, market positioning stability, and people integration. The full essay series is available at [blanewarrene.substack.com](https://blanewarrene.substack.com).
