## Conflict resolution protocol

1. Decision owner attempts resolution with consulted parties
2. If unresolved within 48 hours, escalation owner takes the decision
3. If escalation owner cannot resolve within 72 hours, escalate to executive sponsor
4. Document every escalation in the decision log so patterns surface

## Decision log

Maintain a running log of every escalated decision. Review weekly during the integration. Patterns of escalation indicate where the decision rights matrix needs revision.
